Inspired by Two Very Spoiled Dogs

The Polo and Prince Foundation was born out of love — for our two pups, and for every dog that deserves the same joy. While we're still getting things ready, our goal is simple: to build a platform that helps shelter and street dogs find homes where they’ll be spoiled, safe, and deeply loved — just like Polo and Prince.

What’s Coming

Our full site will feature adoptable dogs, shelter connections, and heartwarming success stories. We're working hard to bring it to life — and we can't wait to share it with you.
